Drawn by the lights & festivities

I was seeing the bright and vivid lights of the west new york carnival while walking to and from the home everyday. I could also hear the upbeat music and surprising Indian songs being played occasionally. Although I knew the rides were not from me and could not see great food options as well, just the brightness and the atmosphere around was so magnetic. When I did make it finally I wasn't disappointed either - the lights and liveliness of the place filled us with joy!
